Returns & Exchanges

Return Policy

We want you to love your artwork! If you're not completely satisfied, we offer:

  • 30-day return window from delivery date
  • Full refund for original artwork in original condition
  • Store credit for prints and gifts
  • Free return shipping for damaged items

What We Cannot Accept

  • Custom commissioned pieces (final sale)
  • Personalized or customized items
  • Items damaged by customer
  • Returns after 30 days

How to Return

  1. Contact us at returns@artwithheartandgifts.com within 30 days
  2. Include your order number and reason for return
  3. We'll provide a return authorization number
  4. Package item securely and ship back
  5. Refund processed within 5-7 business days

Exchanges

Need a different size or style? We're happy to help you find the perfect piece. Exchange requests are handled on a case-by-case basis.
